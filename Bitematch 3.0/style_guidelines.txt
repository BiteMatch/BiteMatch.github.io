Style Guidelines for BiteMatch
===============================

The background for all pages will be the legume.jpg file in images

Colours
========

Navigation: #F6D9C4, white
Text: burlywood
Highlighted text: beige


Typography
==========
Navigation: Satisfy
Text: Staalich


Links
=====
Hovering over links will cause a the text to change to white and a burlywood 
square to surround the text
